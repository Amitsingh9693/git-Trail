Shri Kedarnath Temple

Shri Kedarnath Dham is one of the Char Dhams, located in Uttarakhand. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, it sits at 11,755 feet on the Mandakini River with the majestic Kedarnath Peak behind it.

Baidhyanath Dham Deoghar

Baidhyanath Dham in Deoghar, Jharkhand, is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas. Surrounded by greenery, it is a peaceful place for worship and spiritual reflection.
